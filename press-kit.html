Press Kit

Weather Fit is a unique weather app that takes forecasting to a new level, providing not only weather updates, but also offering the ultimate solution to the everyday question - 'what should I wear today?'

Embrace the engaging character of Weather Fit. Our fun, style-savvy avatar dresses according to the weather, effectively showing you what to wear during the day. Whether it’s sunglasses for sunny days or an umbrella for rainy ones, our character has you covered!

Highlights

Weather Fit has been carefully designed to ensure a fun, engaging, and informative user experience. Here are some of the highlights:

  • Outfit Planner: Our creatively informative character provides a visual guide of what to wear based on the weather forecast.
  • Home & Lock Screen Widgets: Keep a keen eye on the weather conditions and receive clothing suggestions with our easy-to-view widgets for your iPhone and iPad.
  • Character Customization: Customize your avatar's sex, skin tone, hairstyle, glasses, and beard. Make it resemble you or anyone you fancy!
  • Accurate Forecast: Get up-to-date information about the current and future weather conditions, all in one place.
  • Weather Visualization: Our app provides a visual treat with a changing landscape that represents the current weather, time of day, and season.
  • Additional Features: Modify your preference for dressing lighter or warmer, get a minute-by-minute precipitation forecast, and stay informed about air quality index, weather warnings, moon phases, and more. All available in 14 languages.

Pricing

Weather Fit follows a subscription model. Our freemium app is available with the option to upgrade to premium features at a minimal cost. Try our 7-day free trial and enjoy the freedom to cancel anytime.

Premium ($1.99/month, $11.99/year, $39.99/lifetime) includes an ad-free experience, personalized clothes and landscapes, access to all widgets, weather maps and radar, and the ability to switch between Apple Weather, AerisWeather, and AccuWeather for a more accurate forecast. Please note, pricing may vary in different countries.
