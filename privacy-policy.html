Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 16, 2024

Weather Fit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Weather Fit application ("Weather Fit" or "Application"). By using the Application, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect and use the following information to provide, understand, and improve Weather Fit:

  • User Interaction Data
    • Distinct IDs and anonymized device IDs.
    • Usage of Weather Fit features (e.g., total sessions, session length).
    • Preferences for outfit categories (e.g., hats, scarves).
    • Application settings (e.g., language, region, wardrobe settings).
    • Technical data such as iOS version, device model, and app version.
  • Location Data
    • Your device's location to provide localized weather forecasts and outfit suggestions.
    • If location access is disabled, the app may not provide personalized weather predictions.
  • Subscription Information
    • Details about your active or expired subscription plans for Premium features.
  • Aggregated Data
    • Anonymized statistics about app usage, such as how many users access specific features.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts and personalized outfit suggestions.
  • Improve user experience through analytics and feedback.
  • Offer relevant features based on your preferences.
  • Process subscription payments and manage trial periods.
  • Communicate updates or changes to the Application.

Third-Party Services

To enhance functionality, Weather Fit integrates with third-party services that may collect, process, or store certain data. These services include:

  • AdMob (ads)
  • Mixpanel (analytics)
  • Mapbox (mapping features)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Sentry (error tracking and crash reporting)

Each service has its own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review these policies to understand their practices. Weather Fit does not control or assume responsibility for the actions of these third-party services.

Cookies and Tracking

Weather Fit does not directly use cookies. However, third-party services like AdMob may use cookies or similar tracking technologies for personalized ads. Additionally, Weather Fit requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission during the first app launch, which you can manage via your device settings.

Data Sharing and Privacy

Weather Fit takes your privacy seriously. We adhere to strict data protection standards as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Consent to Share Consumption Data with Apple

By using our app and making in-app purchases, you consent to our sharing of data regarding your usage and consumption of purchased content with Apple, as part of our efforts to resolve refund requests. This information may include details about how you have accessed and interacted with the purchased content. Such data sharing is done in compliance with Apple's policies.

Location Data Usage

If you grant access to your device's location:

  • We use it to provide weather forecasts and outfit suggestions.
  • Location data is anonymized and not stored permanently.
  • You can disable location sharing through your device's settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to secure your information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security. By using Weather Fit, you acknowledge that data transmission over the internet carries inherent risks.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for operational purposes. Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.

Data Sources and Accuracy

Weather Fit integrates with multiple weather providers, including Apple Weather, Xweather, and AccuWeather. While we strive for accuracy, discrepancies may occur due to differences in provider data. We do not guarantee the availability or accuracy of forecasts.

International Data Transfers

Weather Fit processes and stores data using third-party services that may be located outside your country. By using the Application, you consent to the transfer, storage, and processing of your information in countries where data protection laws may differ from your local laws.

Third-Party Links

The Application may include links to third-party websites or services. Clicking these links may direct you to their platforms, which have their own privacy policies. Weather Fit is not responsible for the practices or content of third-party services.

Aggregated Data Collection

We may collect anonymous, aggregated statistics (e.g., feature usage rates) to improve the Application. This information cannot identify you and is considered non-personal data.

App Updates and Termination

Weather Fit may require updates to maintain compatibility with iOS or improve functionality. By using the Application, you agree to install updates as needed. We reserve the right to discontinue the Application or certain features at any time. Upon termination, you must cease using the Application.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us at hi@weatherfit.com.
  • Access or update your stored information.

Privacy Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the Application, third-party services, or legal requirements. Continued use of Weather Fit after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact Anton Chuiko at hi@weatherfit.com.
