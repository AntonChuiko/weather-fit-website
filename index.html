Outfit recommendations based on the weather

Your character, dressed for the forecast

Every morning, your character reflects today's weather: shorts when it warms up, a puffer jacket when temperatures drop, sunglasses on sunny afternoons, an umbrella before it rains. It updates for morning, afternoon, evening, and night.

It wears what you actually own

Add your clothes by category: tops, bottoms, outerwear, footwear, accessories. Adjust your comfort level so the app runs warm or cool. Disable items you never wear. The app only ever suggests things you'd actually put on.

The same forecast as your iPhone's weather app

Weather Fit is powered by Apple Weather. Today's forecast, tomorrow's outlook, and the next 10 days, plus minute-by-minute precipitation, UV index, wind, humidity, air quality, moon phase, sunrise and sunset. Plus weather warnings and alerts when severe conditions are forecast.

Questions, answered

  • What should I wear today?

    Open Weather Fit and you'll see today's outfit in a single glance. Your character is dressed in real clothes from your wardrobe, matched to the actual forecast. No temperatures to decode, no guessing. Just look at what your character's wearing and head out.

  • What should I wear today based on the weather?

    Weather Fit reads today's forecast and dresses your character in clothes you actually own. You see the outfit before you leave the house, and it updates through the day as conditions change.

  • What type of clothes should I wear today?

    Weather Fit picks the right layers for the day: t-shirt or sweater, shorts or pants, jacket or none. It tunes them to your personal comfort level so the call fits how warm or cold you run. Everything it suggests comes from clothes you already own.

  • How should I dress for today's weather?

    Weather Fit combines today's forecast with your wardrobe and a comfort level you control. Run warm and it suggests lighter layers; run cold and it bundles up sooner. Your character only ever wears things you actually own, not generic advice.

  • Should I wear shorts today?

    Open Weather Fit and your character will tell you. The app reads today's temperature, factors in your personal comfort level, and shows you whether shorts are the right call, or whether you'll want pants. It also covers tomorrow and the next 10 days, so no more guessing the night before.

  • Do I need a jacket today?

    Weather Fit shows your character dressed for today's weather. If a jacket is needed, your character is wearing one. If not, they're not. No math, no second-guessing.

  • Can I wear a dress today?

    Weather Fit weighs temperature, wind, and rain to tell you whether it's a dress day, and whether you'll want a layer over it. Add your dresses and skirts to your wardrobe and your character wears them whenever the weather's right.

  • Is Weather Fit free?

    Yes. The core app is free with no time limit: forecasts, outfit recommendations, your character, and one home screen widget. The free version includes light in-app ads. Premium unlocks extra widgets, landscapes, the Apple Watch app, unlimited wardrobe items, and removes ads.
